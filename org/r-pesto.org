** pesto

  [[[file:0-recipe-index.org][main recipe page]]]

category: [[[file:c-italian.org][italian]]]

#+begin_verse
 2 cups fresh basil 
 1/4 cup pine nuts
 3 cloves of garlic
 1 tsp salt
 1/3 cup olive oil
 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
 optional:
 lemon juice
 parsley
 pepper
#+end_verse

 in blender, combine all but cheese, until smooth.  stir in cheese.
