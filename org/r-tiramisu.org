#+begin_verse
 lady fingers: layer and moisten with coffee
 multiply as needed: 1 egg, 100g moscarpone, 2 tbsp sugar

 whip egg whites until fluffy.  beat yolk, sugar, cheese.  fold in
 whites.  make layers.  sprinkle with cocoa.  freeze.
#+end_verse
